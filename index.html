About ELENJI

ELENJI HRS is a premier collection of luxury retreats, with our flagship properties nestled among the misty highlands of Kerala. Founded in 1990, we have established ourselves as the embodiment of refined elegance and authentic hospitality in one of India's most breathtaking landscapes.

Our flagship property seamlessly blends contemporary luxury with the rich cultural heritage of Kerala, offering guests a sanctuary where verdant tea plantations meet cloud-kissed mountains. Each properties are thoughtfully designed to frame the region's natural splendor while providing world-class comfort and personalized service.

At ELENJI HRS , we invite guests to experience the enchantment of the Western Ghats , elegent wild life and nature where luxury meets the unspoiled beauty of Kerala's emerald highlands.
