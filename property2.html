About the Homestay

Welcome to ELENJI Dream Villa Homestay in beautiful Munnar tea country. Our cozy property offers 4 comfortable rooms with stunning mountain views. Each room blends traditional Kerala design with modern comforts.

Our family-run homestay provides a peaceful retreat in the Western Ghats where you can enjoy nature walks and the cool mountain climate.

Our friendly hosts are happy to recommend local restaurants and guide you to Munnar's best attractions, including waterfalls and tea estates. Experience the authentic charm of Kerala's highlands at ELENJI Dream Villa Homestay.
