About the Resort

ELENJI Merry Woods Resort welcomes you to our peaceful retreat in Munnar's misty tea plantations. Our property features 22 comfortable rooms , with private balconies offering beautiful views of the Western Ghats and green valleys.

Enjoy our swimming pool that seems to blend with the mountain horizon as you relax in the cool highland air. we're perfectly positioned for guests to explore the natural beauty of Munnar.

Our friendly staff can arrange guided wilderness trails and help you discover the best local experiences. At ELENJI Merry Woods Resort, we provide a comfortable base for your perfect mountain getaway in Kerala's stunning tea country.
